## Global Winner

**Sketchbook Ridge wins the global championship.**

Both judges picked Ridge by a narrow margin. The shared verdict: **The Sketchbook Summit** is the safer production doctrine, but **Sketchbook Ridge** is the stronger playable game pitch while staying close enough to the repo's architecture.

Ridge wins because its first playable hour is easier to imagine:

- a single clockwise ridge loop
- a visible Relay Spire destination
- Trail Card overlays before entering mini-games
- explicit bridge-owned progress facts
- Stampede Sketch as a low-input first new mini-game
- Circuit as an alternate finale key

## Per-Finalist Notes

### The Sketchbook Summit

Strongest idea: production spine. Three zones, Stickers, played/shiny stamps, scene-owned mini-games, bridge-owned progress, no premature framework.

Weakest assumption: its gameplay pitch is slightly more managerial than electric.

Best ideas to steal:

- Receipt Glider
- Contact Peak / summit framing
- Potassium as the done mini-game benchmark
- one-scene/one-toy discipline
- no shared abstractions until repeated pain proves need

### The Sketchbook Archipelago

Strongest idea: earned Stickers visibly coloring in the monochrome world.

Weakest assumption: it overlaps Potassium by adding another ricochet mini-game and proposes `SingleVerbArcadeRuntime` too early.

Best ideas to steal:

- stickers coloring the overworld/background
- portfolio layout as secret map
- low-cortisol movement language
- tap-to-flap / hold-to-glide simplicity

### Sketchbook Ridge

Strongest idea: most playable-feeling finalist. The Relay Spire, one clockwise trail, Trail Cards, shortcut rewards, and Stampede Sketch are concrete and mobile-plausible.

Weakest assumption: the full slate is still too large if treated as a commitment.

Best ideas to steal:

- single side-view ridge strip
- Relay Spire
- Trail Cards before scene entry
- explicit bridge state shape
- Stampede Sketch as first new low-input mini-game
- manual pages as glossary rows instead of a new system

## Reject Or Defer

- No `SingleVerbArcadeRuntime` yet.
- No second ricochet game while Potassium Slip owns that lane.
- No full fighting-game engine, combo engine, or Hollow Knight-style combat branch now.
- No obscure gesture secrets without visible hints and mobile equivalents.
- Do not build every finalist mini-game. Ship the Ridge/Summit overworld spine, Potassium integration, and one new low-input mini-game first.
