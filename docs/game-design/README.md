# Game Design

This directory separates shipped player-facing behavior from future design concepts.

## Documents

- **[Player Manual](./PLAYER_MANUAL.md)**: Current shipped controls, rooms, interactions, return behavior, and playtest notes.
- **[Potassium Slip Manual](./POTASSIUM_SLIP.md)**: Focused guide for the Potassium Slip mini-game.
- **[Brainstorming](./BRAINSTORMING.md)**: Concept notes for "The Artist's Journey" and the "Lens of Perspective".
- **[Level Design & Progression](./LEVELS.md)**: Future zone and progression concepts.
- **[Secret Mini-Games & Homages](./SECRETS_AND_MINIGAMES.md)**: Future hidden mini-game concepts and access ideas.
- **[Core Gameplay Mechanics](./MECHANICS.md)**: Future mechanic ideas for style phasing, triggers, and upgrades.

## Core Pillars

1. **Discovery:** The world is full of hidden artifacts that change how you see and interact with it.
2. **Evolution:** The game starts as a raw sketch and becomes a polished product through player action.
3. **Fun & Surprise:** Secrets, mini-games, and "glitches" keep the experience engaging.

When a concept becomes shipped behavior, update `PLAYER_MANUAL.md` first. Keep speculative material labeled as concept/future design so agents do not treat it as current implementation.
