# Secret Mini-Games & Homages

These are "distilled" versions of classic genres, hidden throughout the world. Each captures the "fun core" of the reference game without requiring full-scale development.

## 1. The "Potassium" Platformer (Mario Homage)
*   **The Vibe:** High-speed 2D platforming.
*   **The Secret:** Find a **Banana** item in the "Sketch" world. Instead of eating it, the player "enters" the banana (it becomes a door).
*   **The Twist:** Everything is banana-themed. The blocks, the clouds, and even the "Goal Pole" which is just a giant banana.
*   **Fun Core:** Tight jumping, a single power-up (e.g., "Banana Peel" dash), and a clear "Goal" at the end.

## 2. Muay Thai Mayhem (Street Fighter Homage)
*   **The Vibe:** 1v1 Side-on combat.
*   **The Secret:** Hidden in the **Hobby Room**. Interact with the heavy bag while wearing the "Glasses." It reveals a spectral AI opponent.
*   **The Twist:** The "AI" is literally a floating brain or a robot with "TODO: Implement AI" written on its chest.
*   **Fun Core:** Simple 3-button combat (Jab, Kick, Block) and a "Special Meter" for a Muay Thai Knee strike.

## 3. The "Infinite" Draft (Dead Cells/Rogue-lite Homage)
*   **The Vibe:** Procedural combat and platforming.
*   **The Secret:** Enter a "Glitchy" elevator in the **Basement**.
*   **The Twist:** The level is "drawn" as you move. It uses a simple tile-based generator. The player fights "Bugs" (literal insect-like entities).
*   **Fun Core:** Fast combat, collecting "Code Snippets" (temporary power-ups), and seeing how far you can get before a "System Crash" (Game Over).

## 4. Survivor's Guilt (Vampire Survivors Homage)
*   **The Vibe:** Top-down wave survival.
*   **The Secret:** Stand still in a specific "Dark Patch" of the city for 10 seconds. The world fades to a top-down view.
*   **The Twist:** Instead of monsters, you are swarmed by **"Deadline"** notifications and **"Zoom"** icons.
*   **Fun Core:** Auto-firing weapons, satisfying "XP" (Coffee Beans) collection, and massive swarms of enemies that "pop" with satisfying effects.

## 5. Ball x Pit (Ball Cross Pit Homage)
*   **The Vibe:** Survival Roguelite / Brick-breaking Hybrid.
*   **The Secret:** Interaction with a specific "Old TV" in the outskirts while holding a "Circuit" item.
*   **The Look:** A vertical "Pit" view. The player is at the top/center, launching physics-based balls into a swarm of enemies below.
*   **The Twist:** As you hit enemies, you collect "Data Bits" that allow you to **Fuse** your ball in real-time (e.g., splitting into 3, adding fire trails, or becoming a "Gravity Ball" that curves).
*   **Fun Core:** Ricochet-based combat (balls bouncing off walls and enemies), high-sensory "crunchy" sound effects, and the satisfying chaos of 50+ balls bouncing around the pit at once.

## Accessing the Secrets (The "Glitch" System)
To keep these secret but reachable, we'll use the **"Glitch Aesthetic"**:
- **Visual Cues:** A specific object will slightly "jitter" or "flicker" between Sketch and Pixel modes.
- **Auditory Cues:** A bit-crushed sound effect when near a secret entrance.
- **The "Dev Menu" Hint:** If the player finds the "Dev Console" secret, they get a cryptic list of "Coordinates" to these games.
